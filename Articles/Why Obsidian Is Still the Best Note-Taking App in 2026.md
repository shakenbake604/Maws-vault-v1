I have tested out many productivity apps this year as I do every year, and this year, as every other year I have been reviewing products, Obsidian is the best overall note-taking app. I would say that this year it is even better given the fact that they have added databases. People have been big fans of [Obsidian’s](https://obsidian.md/) philosophy around note-taking, and the plugin ecosystem has made it so that Obsidian can become anything you want it to be. It is such a versatile application, and people love Obsidian for that, as well as the fact that your files are yours and they live on your device in a format that has been around for a while and will continue to be around. Having all of your markdown files on your device means that you own your files, which has been important and continues to be important for a lot of people. But just because Obsidian is really good does not mean that it is for everyone. I personally struggle to use Obsidian, and I want to start off by highlighting some of the reasons why you would consider not using Obsidian.
