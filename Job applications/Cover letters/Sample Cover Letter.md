# Sample Cover Letter Template

Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to express my interest in the [Position Title] position at [Company Name]. With my background in [your field] and passion for [relevant area], I am excited about the opportunity to contribute to your team.

## Why I'm a Great Fit

- **Experience**: [Brief description of relevant experience]
- **Skills**: [Key skills that match the job requirements]
- **Achievements**: [Notable accomplishments]

## Why [Company Name]

I am particularly drawn to [Company Name] because of [specific reasons - company values, products, culture, etc.]. I admire [specific achievement or aspect of the company].

## What I Can Contribute

In my previous role, I [specific accomplishment with metrics if possible]. I believe these experiences have prepared me well to [how you'll contribute to the company].

I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my skills and experiences align with the needs of your team. Thank you for considering my application.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]

---

## Tips for Writing Cover Letters

1. Research the company thoroughly
2. Customize each letter for the specific role
3. Use specific examples and metrics
4. Show enthusiasm for the company and role
5. Keep it concise (under one page)
6. Proofread carefully
